Cinderella Story

Cinderella is a kind girl forced into servitude by her cruel stepmother and stepsisters, who call her "Cinderella" because she's always covered in soot; a royal ball offers hope, but they prevent her from going, until her fairy godmother magically provides a gown, carriage, and glass slippers, warning her to return by midnight.
At the ball, she captivates the Prince, but flees at midnight, losing a slipper; the Prince uses it to find her, it fits perfectly, and they marry, living happily ever after.
