BIRYANI

A flavorful rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables.
It is slow-cooked to blend rich flavors and often served with raita or salad.

QORMA

A traditional South Asian curry made with meat cooked in yogurt, oil, and spices.
It has a thick, rich gravy and is usually eaten with naan or rice.

FRIED CHICKEN

Chicken pieces coated in seasoned batter and deep-fried until crispy.
It is crunchy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

BURGERS

A sandwich made with a cooked patty placed inside a bun with sauces and vegetables.
Common fillings include beef, chicken, or veggie patties.

PIZZA

An Italian dish with a baked flatbread base topped with sauce, cheese, and toppings.
It comes in many flavors like pepperoni, chicken, and vegetables.

PASTA

An Italian food made from wheat dough, cooked and mixed with sauces.
Popular sauces include tomato, cream, and pesto.

CHOWMEIN

A stir-fried noodle dish mixed with vegetables and meat or chicken.
It is popular for its savory taste and quick cooking style.

BEEF CHILLI

A spicy dish made with beef cooked in chili sauce, soy sauce, and vegetables.
It has a bold, slightly tangy flavor and is often served with rice or noodles.

RAMEN

A Japanese noodle soup served in a flavorful broth with toppings.
Common toppings include eggs, meat, and vegetables.

KIMCHI

A traditional Korean side dish made from fermented vegetables, mainly cabbage.
It has a spicy, sour taste and is rich in probiotics.
